Our newsletter this week highlights a popular topic in the podcasting world: live podcasting and social audio.
Check out our complete guide on how to start your own live podcast!
Podcasts, especially audio podcasts, are the descendants of radio talk shows. And while some elements stay the same, podcasting has grown past these boundaries to allow listeners to tune into podcasts anytime, anywhere. With live podcasts, listeners can engage with you in real time, donate money to your livestream, and be an even deeper part of your podcast’s community.

We’ve put together a complete guide on how to start your own live podcast. Check out our “ How To Start a Live Podcast” guide for starting your own live podcast livestream!
